Objective: The goal is to be the first player to reach or exceed a certain number of points, usually 100.
Gameplay: Players take turns rolling the die. On a turn, a player rolls the die and accumulates points as follows:
- If the player rolls a 1, they score no points for that turn, and it becomes the next player's turn.
- If the player rolls a number other than 1, they can choose to:
       End their turn and keep the points rolled during that turn.
       Roll the die again and potentially accumulate more points.
Accumulating Points : As long as the player continues to roll numbers other than 1, they keep accumulating points on that turn. They can roll as many times as they want on their turn until they choose to end their turn or roll a 1.
Ending Turn: A player can choose to end their turn at any point before rolling a 1. If they do so, they add all the points accumulated during that turn to their total score.
Rolling a 1: If a player rolls a 1, they lose all the points accumulated during that turn, and their turn ends. It becomes the next player's turn.
Winning the Game: The first player to reach or exceed the target score (usually 100) wins the game. If multiple players reach the target score in the same round, the player with the highest score wins.
